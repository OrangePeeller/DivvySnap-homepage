Overview

DivvySnap is a mobile application designed to simplify the process of splitting bills from receipts. Simply snap a photo of your receipt, and the app will automatically recognize the items and calculate how much each person owes. It's perfect for group dining, shared expenses, and more.

Core Features

  • Automatic Receipt Recognition: Uses OCR technology to recognize the receipt content and extract itemized data like prices and quantities.
  • Expense Splitting: Allows users to split the total bill equally or by specific percentages among multiple people.
  • Receipt Storage: Keep a history of all receipts and bills within the app for future reference.
  • Cross-Platform Sync: Available on both iOS and Android, ensuring your data is synchronized across all devices.

Technology Stack

DivvySnap leverages advanced technologies to provide a smooth user experience and efficient processing:

  • OCR Technology: Uses Tesseract OCR for text recognition, enabling the app to accurately extract data from receipts in various languages.
  • Cloud Storage: All data is securely stored on AWS, ensuring high availability and data protection.
  • RESTful API: Our backend services provide RESTful APIs for smooth communication between the app and cloud servers.
  • React Native: Built with React Native for a seamless cross-platform experience on both iOS and Android.
